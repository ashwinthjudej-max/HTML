Briyani

Biryani is a rich, aromatic rice dish made by layering long-grain basmati rice with spiced meat (chicken/mutton/fish) or vegetables, then cooking it slowly so all the flavors mix together. It’s famous for its strong masala taste, saffron/rose aroma, fried onions, and herbs like mint and coriander.

List Of Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chillies
  • Masala
  • Curd
  • Water
  • Whole Spices
  • Ghee / oil
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice
  • Mint Leaves
  • Cashews / raisins
  • Coriender

Steps in Making Briyani

  1. Wash & soak rice Wash basmati rice and soak for 20–30 minutes
  2. Marinate the chicken/mutton Mix meat with curd, ginger-garlic paste, chilli powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, mint, coriander, lemon and rest 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the rice Boil water with whole spices + salt, add rice and cook until 70% done, then drain.
  4. Layer in a pot Add meat gravy at bottom → add rice layer → add fried onions + mint + coriander + saffron milk/ghee.
  5. Dum cook Close lid tight and cook on low flame 20–25 minutes. Rest 10 minutes, then serve.
