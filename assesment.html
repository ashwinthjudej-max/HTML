About The Event

Good morning everyone! Welcome to Pongal Festival 2026. Pongal is one of Tamil Nadu’s most traditional and well-established festivals, celebrating harvest, gratitude, and togetherness.
Today’s event is organized to enjoy our culture, share happiness, and create beautiful memories.
We have planned fun activities like Pongal making, competitions, and lunch together. So let’s celebrate Pongal with energy, smiles, and unity. Thank you all for joining!
